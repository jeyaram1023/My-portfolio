Legal

Privacy Policy

Transparency is our priority. Last updated: January 2026.

1. No Backend & Offline Operation

**Sensor Bridge** functions entirely **offline**. We do not have a backend server, cloud database, or API that collects your data. All sensor data processing, voice recognition, and command generation happen locally on your smartphone's processor. Your project data never leaves your device.

2. Permissions & Usage

We request sensitive permissions solely to enable specific hardware features. Here is exactly how we use them:

Location (Fine/Coarse)

Required for Bluetooth Scanning. Android mandates location permission to detect nearby BLE devices (like your ESP32). We do not track, store, or share your physical GPS location.

Microphone

Voice Command Module. Used exclusively to convert your spoken words into text commands locally. Audio is processed in real-time and never recorded.

Send SMS

GSM/Alert Module. Used only when you explicitly configure the app to send an alert string to a specific number. We never read your inbox.

Camera

Optical Sensors. Used for features like Color Detection or Flashlight control. Video feeds are processed locally for sensor values and are never saved.

3. Children's Privacy

While this app is an educational tool suitable for students of all ages, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app works offline and has no account system, no personal data profiles are created.
